Short biography

"Every morning when I wake up, thank you for the life that gave me a day to smile and love"

My hometown is Long An, originally from the West. I come from a quite harmonious and peaceful family. When I was young, I was bullied by my brother. My brother's personality was so quick, so I was shy.

In the past, I had two very close friends, they were very gentle and very helpful to me in study and in life. But time does not spare anyone, growing up, each of them has a life of their own, an other close friend.

We soon contact with Buddha when encountering so many obstacles and ups and downs in relationships. For example, when I went to college, I met and got close with many friends, but due to my selfishness and persistence, the relationship was almost cracked. Through contact with the Dharma, knowing how to cultivate the mind and nature, I am now very popular with my friends, from inside to outside.
